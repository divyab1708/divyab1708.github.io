About us

Grex is an online platform to invest in startups

We provide an online platform for discovering and investing in great startups.

We make it possible for people to invest as much or as little as they like in the startups they choose through a simple, online process, and we let startup businesses raise seed capital seamlessly from friends, family, members of their communities and independent investors.
